## Information Science — Job Titles and Related Skills

### Information Architect
- Metadata design & schema development  
- Taxonomy & ontology creation  
- Information architecture (site/app navigation)  
- Content strategy & content modeling  
- User research & card sorting  
- Wireframing & prototyping  
- Accessibility standards (WCAG)  
- Information retrieval fundamentals

### Data Analyst
- SQL & relational database querying  
- Data cleaning & ETL processes  
- Exploratory data analysis (EDA)  
- Statistical methods & hypothesis testing  
- Data visualization (Tableau, matplotlib, D3)  
- Scripting (Python, R)  
- Reporting & dashboard design  
- Data governance basics

### Data Engineer
- Database design & data modeling  
- ETL pipeline development  
- NoSQL databases & data lakes  
- Workflow orchestration (Airflow, Luigi)  
- API design & integration  
- Performance tuning & indexing  
- Cloud data services (AWS/GCP/Azure)

### Knowledge Manager / Knowledge Engineer
- Knowledge capture & curation  
- Ontology & controlled vocabularies  
- Content lifecycle & preservation  
- Taxonomy management tools  
- Internal search tuning & relevance feedback  
- Documentation & knowledge base design  
- Stakeholder interviewing & training

### Information Retrieval / Search Engineer
- Search algorithms & indexing (Lucene, Elasticsearch)  
- Query parsing & relevance ranking  
- Natural language processing (NLP) basics  
- Faceted search & filtering design  
- Synonym/stopword handling & stemming  
- A/B testing & search analytics  
- Performance/scalability tuning

### UX Researcher (Information-focused)
- User interviews & contextual inquiry  
- Usability testing & task analysis  
- Information-seeking behavior studies  
- Card sorting & tree testing  
- Persona & journey mapping  
- Qualitative & quantitative research methods  
- Reporting insights to product teams

### Digital Archivist / Digital Preservation Specialist
- Digital preservation standards & practices  
- Metadata standards (Dublin Core, PREMIS)  
- File format identification & migration strategies  
- Archival description & cataloging  
- Repository management systems  
- Rights management & access policies  
- Backup, fixity checks, and integrity validation

### Library / Resource Services Manager
- Cataloging & classification (MARC, LCSH)  
- Reference services & information literacy instruction  
- Collection development & curation  
- Library systems (ILS) & discovery tools  
- User education & workshops  
- Licensing & digital resource access management

### Information Policy Analyst
- Privacy & data protection principles  
- Information governance & compliance  
- Intellectual property & licensing basics  
- Policy writing & impact assessment  
- Ethical AI & algorithmic accountability  
- Stakeholder consultation & advocacy

### Product Manager — Information Products
- Requirements gathering & stakeholder management  
- Roadmapping for search/content products  
- Metrics/KPI definition (engagement, relevance)  
- Wireframing & feature specification  
- Cross-functional coordination with engineering & UX  
- Competitive analysis & market research

### Machine Learning / NLP Engineer (entry-level skills)
- Text preprocessing & feature extraction  
- Basic supervised/unsupervised models (classification, clustering)  
- Word embeddings & language models fundamentals  
- Evaluation metrics (precision, recall, F1)  
- Experimentation & model validation  
- Familiarity with ML libraries (scikit-learn, TensorFlow, PyTorch)

### Records Manager / Compliance Specialist
- Records lifecycle management & retention schedules  
- Audit trails & provenance tracking  
- Regulatory compliance (e.g., GDPR basics)  
- Access controls & permissions models  
- Policy implementation & training
